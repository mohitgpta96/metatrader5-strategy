1 Strategy Overview

▼

Yeh Strategy Kya Hai?

"Triple Confirmation" ka matlab hai ki hum 3 alag indicators se confirm karte hain ki trade lena chahiye ya nahi. Jab tak teeno indicators green signal nahi dete, tab tak koi trade nahi lena hai. Isse false signals bahut kam ho jaate hain.

3 Indicators

EMA 20 / 50
RSI 14
ATR 14
Indicator Kya Karta Hai Kaise Use Karna Hai
EMA 20 Short-term trend dikhata hai (20 candles ka average) Price direction aur crossover ke liye
EMA 50 Medium-term trend dikhata hai (50 candles ka average) EMA 20 ke saath crossover dekhna
RSI 14 Overbought/Oversold batata hai (0-100 scale) Confirmation + extreme levels avoid karna
ATR 14 Average volatility batata hai (kitna move hota hai) Stop Loss aur Take Profit calculate karna

2 Signal Types

Type Naam Kab Milta Hai
Type 1 EMA Crossover Jab EMA 20 aur EMA 50 cross karte hain — naya trend start
Type 2 Trend Pullback Jab price existing trend mein EMA 20 tak wapas aata hai — re-entry
Timeframes

Primary chart: 1H (1 hour) candles par trade lete hain

Confirmation: 4H (4 hour) chart se overall trend confirm karte hain

4H chart par EMA 20 > EMA 50 ho toh bullish trend hai, EMA 20 < EMA 50 ho toh bearish.

2 BUY Signal Rules

▼

BUY signal tab aata hai jab market upar jaane wala hota hai. Dono types mein teeno indicators ka confirmation zaroori hai.

▲ Type 1: EMA Crossover BUY
  • EMA 20 ne EMA 50 ko neeche se upar cross kiya (Golden Cross)
  • RSI value 40 se 70 ke beech ho (na oversold, na overbought)
  • 4H chart pe overall trend bullish ho (EMA 20 > EMA 50 on 4H)

Kab hota hai? Jab naya uptrend start hota hai. EMA 20 fast hai, EMA 50 slow. Jab fast wala slow ke upar jaata hai, matlab buyers control mein aa rahe hain.

▲ Type 2: Trend Pullback BUY
  • Price pehle se uptrend mein ho (EMA 20 already above EMA 50)
  • Price neeche aake EMA 20 ko touch kare ya uske paas aaye (pullback)
  • RSI value 35 se 55 ke beech ho (slightly oversold zone)
  • Price EMA 20 se bounce back kare (wapas upar jaane lage)

Kab hota hai? Uptrend mein price kabhi kabhi thoda neeche aata hai (pullback). EMA 20 support ki tarah kaam karta hai. Jab price wahin se bounce kare, toh entry milti hai. Yeh Type 1 se zyada reliable hota hai.

Stop Loss & Take Profit (BUY)

Stop Loss
Entry - 1.5x ATR
R:R Ratio
1:1.33 & 1:2.0
Take Profit 1
Entry + 2.0x ATR
Take Profit 2
Entry + 3.0x ATR
Example: Gold BUY Trade

Entry: $5,140 | ATR(14): $40

Stop Loss: $5,140 - (1.5 x $40) = $5,080 (60 points neeche)

TP1: $5,140 + (2.0 x $40) = $5,220 (80 points upar, R:R = 1:1.33)

TP2: $5,140 + (3.0 x $40) = $5,260 (120 points upar, R:R = 1:2.0)

TP1 hit hone par half position close karo, baaki TP2 ke liye rakho. SL ko breakeven par move karo.

3 SELL Signal Rules

▼

SELL signal tab aata hai jab market neeche jaane wala hota hai. BUY ka mirror image hai — sab ulta ho jaata hai.

▼ Type 1: EMA Crossover SELL
  • EMA 20 ne EMA 50 ko upar se neeche cross kiya (Death Cross)
  • RSI value 30 se 60 ke beech ho
  • 4H chart pe overall trend bearish ho (EMA 20 < EMA 50 on 4H)

Kab hota hai? Jab naya downtrend start hota hai. EMA 20 neeche girta hai EMA 50 ke, matlab sellers control mein aa rahe hain.

▼ Type 2: Trend Pullback SELL
  • Price pehle se downtrend mein ho (EMA 20 already below EMA 50)
  • Price upar jaake EMA 20 ko touch kare (pullback/bounce up)
  • RSI value 45 se 65 ke beech ho (slightly overbought zone)
  • Price EMA 20 se reject hoke neeche gire (wapas neeche jaane lage)

Kab hota hai? Downtrend mein price thoda upar aata hai (pullback). EMA 20 resistance ki tarah kaam karta hai. Jab price wahin se reject ho, toh SELL entry milti hai.

4 Risk Management

▼
SABSE IMPORTANT SECTION

Strategy se zyada important hai risk management. Best strategy bhi bina proper risk management ke account blow kar degi. Yeh rules kabhi mat todo.

Core Rules

  1. 1% Risk Per Trade: Har trade mein maximum account ka 1% risk karo. $10,000 account = max $100 loss per trade.
  2. Max 1 Open Trade: Ek time pe sirf 1 hi trade open rakho. Multiple trades = multiple risk. Beginner ke liye 1 kafi hai.
  3. Daily Loss Limit 3%: Agar ek din mein 3% loss ho jaaye ($300 on $10K), toh us din ka trading BAND. Computer band karo.
  4. Weekly Loss Limit 5%: Agar ek week mein 5% loss ho jaaye ($500 on $10K), toh poore hafte ka trading BAND. Review karo kya galat hua.

Position Sizing Formula

Yeh formula batata hai ki kitne lots trade karne hain taaki risk 1% se zyada na ho:

// Step 1: Risk amount nikalo risk_amount = account_balance x 0.01 // Example: $10,000 x 0.01 = $100 // Step 2: SL distance nikalo (ATR se) sl_distance = 1.5 x ATR(14) // Example: 1.5 x $40 = $60 // Step 3: Lot size calculate karo lots = risk_amount / (sl_distance x contract_size) // Gold: $100 / ($60 x 100) = 0.0167 lots // Round down: 0.01 lots

Contract Sizes

Market Contract Size $1 Move = Max Lots ($10K)
Gold (XAUUSD) 100 oz per lot $100 per lot 0.03 lots
Silver (XAGUSD) 5,000 oz per lot $5,000 per lot 0.01 lots
Indian Stocks Varies Varies Calculate per stock
Gold Hard Cap

Gold ke liye hard cap: 0.05 lots per $1,000 balance. $10K account = maximum 0.05 lots overall. But recommended hai 0.03 lots ya usse kam.

Formula chahe 0.05 lots bole, toh bhi 0.03 se zyada mat lo.

KABHI MAT KARO (Never Do)

  • KABHI Stop Loss hatao ya door mat karo — SL lagaya hai toh wahin rehne do, chahe loss ho raha ho. SL hatana = account blow.
  • KABHI NFP ya FOMC ke time trade mat karo — Non-Farm Payroll (har mahine first Friday) aur Federal Reserve meetings ke time Gold $50-100 ek minute mein hil sakta hai. Calendar check karo.
  • KABHI revenge trading mat karo — Loss ke baad gusse mein bada trade lena = zyada loss. Agar 3% daily limit touch ho jaaye, band karo.
  • KABHI apna poora capital ek trade mein mat lagao — "All in" sirf gambling hai, trading nahi.
  • KABHI bina Stop Loss ke trade mat karo — SL nahi lagaya toh unlimited loss ho sakta hai. Trade kholte hi SL lagao.

5 Trade Checklist

▼

Har trade se pehle yeh checklist check karo. Agar koi bhi item ✗ hai, toh trade mat lo.

Entry Checklist

  • ✓ Higher Timeframe Trend: 4H chart pe EMA 20/50 se trend confirm kiya? BUY ke liye bullish, SELL ke liye bearish hona chahiye.
  • ✓ Signal Type: Kya Type 1 (Crossover) ya Type 2 (Pullback) signal clearly dikh raha hai 1H chart pe?
  • ✓ RSI Range: Kya RSI sahi range mein hai? (BUY T1: 40-70, BUY T2: 35-55, SELL T1: 30-60, SELL T2: 45-65)
  • ✓ Stop Loss Calculated: ATR se SL calculate kiya? 1.5x ATR distance hai?
  • ✓ 1% Risk Check: Position size formula se lots calculate kiye? Risk $100 se zyada toh nahi? (on $10K)
  • ✓ Daily Loss Limit: Aaj ka total loss 3% se kam hai? Agar 3% touch ho gaya toh aaj trade NAHI.
  • ✓ Weekly Loss Limit: Is hafte ka total loss 5% se kam hai? Agar 5% touch ho gaya toh is hafte trade NAHI.
  • ✓ News Check: Kya aaj koi major news hai? (NFP, FOMC, CPI). Agar haan toh news ke 1 hour pehle aur baad trade mat karo.
  • ✓ No Other Open Trade: Kya abhi koi aur trade open hai? Agar haan toh naya trade mat karo (max 1 open).
  • ✓ TP Levels Set: TP1 (2.0x ATR) aur TP2 (3.0x ATR) dono set kiye? TP1 pe half close ka plan hai?
Checklist Tip

Items par click karke check/uncheck kar sakte ho. Har naye trade se pehle sab reset karo aur phir se check karo.

Math Samjho: Account Wipeout

// $10K account, 1 standard lot Gold BUY Daily range = $80 - $100 1 lot exposure = $100 x 100 = $10,000/day potential swing // Agar Gold $100 neeche jaaye (normal day): Loss = 100 x $100 = $10,000 = POORA ACCOUNT KHATAM // Isliye KABHI 1 lot mat lo $10K account pe!
Recommended Lot Size for Gold

$10,000 account ke liye Gold mein maximum 0.03 lots lo.

0.03 lots mein: $1 move = $3 | $100 move (full day) = $300 loss = 3% (daily limit)

Yeh safe hai kyunki daily limit 3% hai aur 0.03 lots se worst case bhi 3% se zyada nahi jaayega ek normal day mein.

Hard Cap yaad rakho: 0.05 lots per $1,000 balance. But 0.03 recommended hai.

Reality Check

Yeh targets average hain. Kuch weeks loss mein bhi jaayenge — yeh normal hai.

Losing weeks se daro mat. Agar rules follow karte raho toh long term mein profit hoga.

Pehle 2-3 months ko learning period samjho. Zyada profit ki expectation mat rakho initially.

Key Points Yaad Rakho

1. Zyada trades mat lo — quality > quantity. 3-4 trades/week kafi hai.

2. Har hafte profit nahi hoga — yeh normal hai. Monthly average dekho.

3. Risk management hi sabse bada edge hai — strategy secondary hai.

4. Patience rakho — compounding ka magic time ke saath dikhta hai.
